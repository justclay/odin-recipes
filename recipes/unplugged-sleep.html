Unplugged Sleep Recipe


Description

The Unplugged Sleep recipe is a soothing formula for achieving restorative slumber in our tech-saturated world. It emphasizes the importance of creating a tranquil sleep environment by turning off electronic devices well before bedtime. This recipe helps to minimize sleep disturbances and encourages a deeper state of rest by leveraging the calming effects of a dark, cool room and the subtle scent of lavender. It’s the perfect remedy for those seeking a peaceful night’s sleep, free from digital interruptions.



Ingredients

  • 1 comfortable bed
  • A pinch of lavender oil or sleep spray
  • A dark, cool room
  • All electronic devices turned off

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle your pillow with a bit of lavender oil or sleep spray.
  2. Ensure your room is dark and at a comfortable temperature.
  3. Turn off all electronic devices at least 30 minutes before bed.
  4. Drift off into a peaceful, uninterrupted night's sleep.
